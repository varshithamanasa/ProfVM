    I am an Assistant Professor of Cybersecurity and Coordinator of Cyber Security at Lasell University, driven by a passion to empower and inspire future technology leaders. With a strong foundation in computer science and cybersecurity, I create innovative curricula, lead transformative research, and mentor students to unlock their full potential in advanced security practices. My expertise spans threat analysis and building collaborative learning environments that foster curiosity, resilience, and excellence. I am dedicated to bridging academia and industry, championing cybersecurity education, and motivating the next generation to make a meaningful impact. As a Women in Security and Privacy advocate and WiCyS Faculty Advisor, I am committed to advancing diversity and inclusion in technology, supporting women and underrepresented groups to thrive in cybersecurity. Beyond technology, I advocate for mental health and wellness, and as a certified yoga instructor, I encourage holistic growth and well-being in every journey.
